About SEVAVRAT FOUNDATION

SEVAVRAT FOUNDATION is a social organization committed to making a tangible difference in the lives of individuals and communities. Our core initiatives include:

  • Women Empowerment and Training: We conduct various training programs for women and girls, fostering empowerment. We also implement other social activities and provide guidance in education, health, and industry.

  • Employment for the Needy: We strive to provide meaningful employment opportunities to individuals in need, helping them achieve financial stability and dignity.

  • Hospital Patient Support: We facilitate blood donations for patients and provide companionship for those without family support. We also offer guidance for managing and paying hospital bills.

  • Support for Old Age Homes and Orphanages: We regularly visit various old age homes and orphanages in and around Pune, organizing new activities, providing essential items and medicines, and offering personal care services like hair cutting for beneficiaries.

  • Writers for Disabled Students: We arrange suitable writers for boys and girls with disabilities for competitive and other examinations, ensuring equal opportunities.

  • Government Scheme Awareness: We disseminate information about government facilities and schemes to common people, especially those in underserved habitats, ensuring they can benefit from these provisions.
